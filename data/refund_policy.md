# Sole Syntax — Official Refund & Return Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Company:** Sole Syntax Inc.  
**Contact:** support@solesyntax.com

---

## 1. Standard Return Window
- Customers have **30 days from the date of delivery** to initiate a return.
- Returns requested after 30 days will be **automatically denied**, no exceptions.

## 2. Condition Requirements
- Items must be **unworn, unaltered, and in original condition**.
- Shoes must be returned in the **original box with all packaging materials**.
- Items showing signs of wear (scuff marks, dirty soles, stretched material) are **not eligible for refund**.
- Tags must be attached and intact.

## 3. Proof of Purchase
- A valid **Order ID** is required for all return requests.
- Without an Order ID, the return **cannot be processed**.

## 4. Sale & Clearance Items
- All items purchased at **20% discount or more are Final Sale** — no returns, no exchanges.
- Clearance items marked "FINAL SALE" are **never eligible** for refund.

## 5. Defective / Damaged Items
- Manufacturing defects reported within **90 days of delivery** are eligible for a full refund or replacement.
- Customer must provide a **photo description** of the defect.
- Damage caused by misuse, improper care, or accidents is **not covered**.

## 6. Loyalty Member Benefits
- **Gold tier members** (annual spend ≥ $500) receive an extended **45-day return window**.
- **Platinum tier members** (annual spend ≥ $1000) receive a **60-day return window** and free return shipping.
- Standard tier members follow the standard 30-day policy.

## 7. Exchanges
- Exchanges for a different size/color are allowed within the same return window.
- Exchanged items follow all the same condition requirements.

## 8. Refund Processing
- Approved refunds are processed within **5–7 business days** to the original payment method.
- Shipping costs are **non-refundable** unless the return is due to a defect or Sole Syntax error.

## 9. Non-Returnable Items
- Gift cards
- Customized / personalized shoes
- Items marked FINAL SALE
- Worn or damaged items
- Items returned without an Order ID

## 10. Escalation
- If a customer disputes a denial, the case can be **escalated to a human supervisor**.
- Supervisors handle exceptions on a case-by-case basis and will respond within 24 hours.
- Supervisor decisions are **final**.

---

*Sole Syntax reserves the right to update this policy at any time. The version in effect at the time of purchase governs the return.*
